# Privacy Policy for YouTube Shorts Redirect

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026

## Data Collection

This extension does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any user data whatsoever.

## Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **`declarativeNetRequest`**: Required to redirect YouTube Shorts URLs to watch URLs at the browser level
- **Host permissions for `*.youtube.com` and `youtu.be`**: Required to detect and redirect YouTube URLs

## What We Do

- Detect YouTube Shorts URLs
- Redirect them to regular YouTube watch URLs
- All processing happens locally in your browser

## What We DON'T Do

- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT track your browsing history
- We do NOT send data to external servers
- We do NOT log or store URLs you visit
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- We do NOT display advertisements

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any third-party services. All functionality is performed entirely within your local browser.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted in this document and the extension will be updated accordingly.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
